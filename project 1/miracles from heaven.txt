<div class="story">
MIRACLES FROM HEAVEN is based on the incredible true story of the Beam family.
 When Christy <b>(Jennifer Garner)</b> discovers her 10-year-old daughter Anna <b>(Kylie Rogers)</b> has a rare, incurable disease, 
she becomes a ferocious advocate for her daughter's healing as she searches for a solution. After Anna has a freak accident, 
an extraordinary miracle unfolds in the wake of her dramatic rescue that leaves medical specialists mystified, her family restored,
 and their community inspired.</div>
